# Legal and Compliance Notes

## Positioning

This POC is framed as collectible NFTs with utility burn mechanics.

## Important Caveats

- This repository is not legal advice.
- Regulatory treatment differs by jurisdiction.
- Marketing language can materially change legal risk profile.

## Recommended Pre-Mainnet Actions

1. Obtain gaming/financial counsel in target jurisdictions.
2. Review KYC/AML obligations where applicable.
3. Define geo-restriction strategy if needed.
4. Finalize terms of service and disclosures.
5. Complete independent smart-contract and security audits.

## Operational Transparency Recommendations

- Publish odds and RNG verification instructions publicly.
- Publish contract source and deployment manifests.
- Maintain incident response and disclosure policy.

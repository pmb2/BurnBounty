# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and professional collaboration environment.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus on technical substance.
- Assume good intent.
- Accept and provide feedback professionally.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, abuse, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or doxxing.
- Deliberate disruption or bad-faith engagement.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or ban contributors for violations.

## Reporting

Report incidents privately to project maintainers.
